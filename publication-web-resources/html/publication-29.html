Virginia Beach to Host 12th Annual

Stonewall Sports 2026 National Tournament

NORFOLK CHAPTER IS ORGANIZING VOLUNTEERS AND FILLING LEADERSHIP ROLES

The energy of the Hampton Roads LGBT community is about to get even more vibrant. Stonewall Sports, Inc., in a proud partnership with the Stonewall Sports Norfolk chapter, has officially announced that the 2026 National Tournament will be held in Virginia Beach, Virginia, from October 22–25, 2026.

This marks a significant “remix” for the organization’s 12th annual flagship event, moving the competition and community festivities to the iconic Virginia oceanfront for a weekend of inclusive sports and philanthropy.

The 2026 National Tournament is expected to draw nearly 3,000 LGBTQIA+ athletes and allies from across the United States. While the tournament is rooted in competitive spirit, its primary mission remains fostering a supportive environment where everyone can be their authentic selves.

The decision to bring the tournament to The Resort City highlights the strength of the local Norfolk chapter and the inclusive growth of the Hampton Roads region.

In its national announcement, Stonewall Sports said, “The Norfolk chapter is inviting you to the gorgeous Hampton Roads, ready to welcome you for a weekend of competition and community.”

Stonewall Sports Norfolk is currently recruiting for the Virginia Beach Tournament Committee. Local volunteers are needed to help coordinate logistics, social events, and hospitality to ensure the thousands of visiting athletes experience the best of Virginia hospitality.

The Norfolk chapter is already building the Virginia Beach Tournament Committee. Whether you want a formal leadership role or just want to help on the

ground during the event, here are the best ways to reach out:

• Email tournament@stonewallsports.org to get on the notification list for setup, event operations, and breakdown.

• Events Committee: If you want to help plan the social side (parties, mixers, the pageant), contact tournament@stonewallsports.org.

• Leadership/Board Roles: For those looking to take on a larger coordination role, you can review available positions and descriptions online.

• Sign-up Form: You can also fill out the interest form directly on the Stonewall Sports Norfolk Volunteer Page.

• Sponsorships are also available.

Learn more at stonewallsports.org/national-tournament.
