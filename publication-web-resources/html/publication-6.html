PHOTOS FROM THE HISTORIC 2026 INAUGURATION PARADE

In the 2026 Virginia Inaugural Parade for Governor Abigail Spanberger, LGBTQ organizations from across the Commonwealth were invited to march. Approximately 75 people made up the contingency as widely noted as a historic shift in tone for the state’s executive branch, as it was the first time such a large, dedicated LGBTQ contingent was officially invited to participate in a Virginia gubernatorial inaugural parade.
